Nuggets

Description

Chicken nuggets is a popular kids friendly recipe made using chicken. Kids favorite recipe which is popular all over the world and it was made popular from all the fast food restaurants. Here I have shared five version of making the best chicken nuggets ever.

  • Chicken
  • cheese
  • Bread flour
  1. Cut the chicken breasts into nuggets.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, cheese, and seasonings.
  3. Dip the nuggets in melted butter, then coat them in the bread crumb mixture.
  4. Bake until the chicken is brown and cooked through.
